## 💡 Key Findings

### Pattern: "website_stream" was accurate descriptor
Most districts labeled "website_stream" DO have actual archived videos on their websites. The term is appropriate but can be refined to "website_archive" for clarity.

### Archive Indicators:
- URL contains "/archive/", "/videos/", or "/_recordings"
- Notes mention "archived", "recordings", or "posted"
- Dedicated video subdomains (video.example.org)
- Reference to video platforms (Vimeo, Mediaspace)

### Livestream-Only Indicators:
- Notes say "livestreamed" without "archived"
- Only mentions "upcoming meetings" or "live stream"
- No evidence of past meeting access

### Ambiguous Cases:
- Pages that say "videos" but could be promotional
- Pages requiring login/authentication
- Pages that mention livestream but don't clarify archive status
